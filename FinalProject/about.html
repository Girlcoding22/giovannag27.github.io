What does E.Coli cause?

The most recent outbreak was in 2018 and California had over 40 people that were ill. E.Coli is manifested in many ways, some common symptoms include: abdominal cramping, gas, loss of appetite, nausea, vomiting, fatigue, fever or sudden diarrhea. Many people recommend at home care which consist of drinking plenty of water, getting a well rest and being aware of your well-being.
