E.Coli


Who we are

Hello, we are four girls and in our design thinking class we came up with a question in hopes that the answer will help our community. The question was “How might we work with scientist to treat contaminated food to stop people from suffering from E.Coli in California?”. How might we help people learn their food to avoid getting infected by E.coli in California? How might we educate California resident about E.coli and to prevent from catching it? Recently we have been seeing the E.Coli disease take a toll in California. We believe that this is something important to know because not only will it reduce the amount of people who have E.Coli but it might change how we take care of ourselves and our food.
